Privacy Policy for DailyInspire

This privacy policy governs your use of the Daily Inspire mobile application.

  1. Introduction

    This privacy policy outlines the types of information collected by Daily Inspire and how it is used. By using Daily Inspire, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

  2. Information Collection and Usage

    While using Daily Inspire, we do not directly collect any personal information from you. However, our app uses Google Mobile Ads, a third-party service, which may collect information used to identify you.

  3. Third-Party Services

    Daily Inspire uses Google Mobile Ads to serve ads within the app. Google Mobile Ads may collect information, such as device information and location data, to serve personalized ads based on your interests and browsing behavior. For more information on Google's privacy practices, please visit their privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

  4. Ad Personalization

    Users may see personalized ads based on their interests and browsing behavior. You can opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings.

  5. Data Sharing

    We do not share any user data with third parties, except as required by Google Mobile Ads to provide advertising services.

  6. Data Retention

    As we do not directly collect user data, we do not retain any user information. Google Mobile Ads may retain data in accordance with their privacy policy.

  7. User Rights and Choices

    Users have the right to access, modify, or delete their information collected by Google Mobile Ads, as outlined in Google's privacy policy.

  8. Security Measures

    We are committed to protecting your privacy and have implemented reasonable security measures to safeguard any information collected by Google Mobile Ads.

  9. Children's Privacy

    Daily Inspire is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by applicable law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

  10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update our privacy policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Your continued use of Daily Inspire after any changes to the privacy policy will constitute your acceptance of those changes.

  11. Contact Us

    If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at GitHub profile.
